About

I'm Tristan! Game developer and player based in Cape Town, South Africa. I love making games, enjoying architectural and system design programming. I love working with user interfaces, experiences and the polishing phase of game creation.

I have developed specific tooling to help me with development. Sentinal has been a life saver for menu-based gamepad support in my most recent Unity games, Pathways has sorted the "where" when saving my game data and Tweening-Components helps give my UI elements ✨animation✨.
